How it works

Three steps to never repeat yourself again

Install once. Mnemox works silently in the background — no new tabs, no copy-pasting.

1

Captures your prompts

Every time you send a prompt to any AI tool, Mnemox saves it as a memory — silently, in the background.

2

Understands semantically

Memories are embedded with OpenAI vectors. When you type a new prompt, Mnemox finds the most relevant past context.

3

Injects context automatically

Before your prompt is sent, Mnemox prepends the most relevant memories. The AI responds like it's known you for months.
